This page curates **public, research-backed resources** on how Microsoft Copilot and related technologies support neurodivergent individuals (e.g. ADHD, autism, dyslexia) through accessibility, cognitive support, and inclusive design.

<br>

## ✅ Key Takeaways

- Copilot helps **reduce cognitive load** by summarizing and structuring information  
- It improves **communication and expression**, especially for dyslexia or ADHD  
- It provides **adaptive, contextual support** across familiar M365 tools  
- Studies show strong impact on **inclusion, productivity, and workplace confidence**
